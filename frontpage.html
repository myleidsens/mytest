Our Mision

CCMH has been a part of the community since it was founded on July 15, 1975 and is the behavioral health provider for Columbia County. We are a Certified Community Behavioral Health Clinicand believe in integrated health care and serve anyone seeking services regardless of yourability to pay, at any age.

We serve everyone across the life span, providing support through all developmental phases, life transitions, and impactful events. Our staff are trained to serve ‘unique populations’, such as veterans, young children, and those experiencing intellectual and developmental disabilities.
